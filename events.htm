1st National Walk for ITP – Pump It Up
with Perpetual Home Support Services
St Louis, MO
2018
Sponsor Commitment

Saturday, September 15, 2018 Perpetual Home Supportive Services (PHSS) will host St. Louis’ 1st Annual National Walk for ITP with the Platelet Disorder Association (PDA) 501c3. ITP stands for immune thrombocytopenia, which is an auto immune disorder which occurs when the blood cell platelet count severely drops. ITP, if not treated, can result in bleeding out and death.

ITP primarily affects women and children and can be debilitating to live with. Currently there is no cure for this disease. The September event will be the 1st ever National Walk for ITP/PDA to take place in the state of Missouri. This event is in honor of a dear friend who suffers from ITP. She is only 25 years old and was diagnosed when she was barely 13 years old and this disorder completely changed her life.

The walk will take place at the Carondalet Park Boathouse. We are seeking monetary, nonmonetary sponsors, and direct partnerships for this great event as it will help PHSS and PDA continue to fight for a cure and help educate those directly and indirectly impacted with this auto immune disorder. We anticipate the total cost for this event to be right at $12,000.00. Marketing and radio promotional ads will cost $7000.00, supplies, equipment, decorations, park rental fees, entertainment/ DJ to host the event, will cost $2000.00, and PHSS will donate a minimum of $3,000.00 to the PDA foundation as the organization does not receive any federal funding. The lack of funding is the reason why we need your support.
