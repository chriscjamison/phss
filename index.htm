What Perpetual Home Supportive Services Does

We are here to assist, in every way, all individuals with special mental and/or physical needs live within the community and not confined in a facility.

What PHSS Does

Perpetual Home and Supportive Services (PHSS) is a 501c3 which provides individualized supportive living services, short term respite care, in home respite care, and shared living supported services to help our consumers maintain and improve independent living skills, and implement supervision and safety measures such as assistive technology to meet the needs of our residents to allow them to remain in the community.

Mission Statement

In March of 2014, Perpetual Home Supportive Services (PHSS) was formed as a 501c3 nonprofit organization with the goal of giving back to the community and low-income residents of the city of St. Louis metro area. PHSS was organized exclusively for charitable, scientific, health, nutrition, recreation, educational, job training, and to assist with the supportive living needs for individuals with intellectual and mental disabilities, and residents who have been stricken with HIV/AIDS.

Services Offered

PHSS offers:

  • Licensed therapy services
  • Nursing care
  • Personal care
  • Budgeting
  • Writing checks
  • Nutrition and cooking
  • Meal planning
  • Shopping
  • Guidance to residents while taking their medications
  • Scheduling medical appointments
